About PyCon SK

2022

The PyCon SK 2022 conference, which will be held in Bratislava, is an annual gathering of the community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. It is organized by volunteers from the SPy o.z., civic association dedicated to advancing and promoting Python and other Open Source technologies and ideas. Through PyCon, the SPy o.z. advances its mission of growing the Slovak (and also international) community of Python programmers and Open Source supporters.


Edu Summit

EduSummit is a special section of the conference focused on teaching programming at primary and secondary schools. We are now organising the fourth edition, a two day summit during which teachers will be introduced to innovative ways and methods of teaching programming, share their knowledge of useful tools and last but not least participate in numerous workshops given by experienced educators.
